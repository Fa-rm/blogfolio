## Step 1: Filtered Water

The first and most important step to making the perfect cup of coffee is to use filtered water. The quality of your water has a significant impact on the taste of your coffee. Make sure to use clean, fresh water that's been filtered before boiling.


## Step 2: Boiling Water

Once you have your filtered water, it's time to boil it. The ideal temperature for brewing coffee is around 200 degrees Fahrenheit (93.3 degrees Celsius). If you don't have a thermometer, you can bring the water to a boil and then let it sit for 30 seconds before brewing.

## Step 3: Grinding

Next, it's time to grind your coffee. For the best flavor, you should grind your coffee beans just before brewing. I recommend using a manual grinder for this step to get the perfect consistency.

## Step 4: Filter

Once you've ground your coffee, it's time to prepare your filter. I recommend using a cool filter, such as a stainless steel or ceramic filter, to prevent the coffee from absorbing any unwanted flavors.

## Step 5: Pouring

Finally, it's time to pour your hot water over your coffee grounds. I recommend pouring the water by hand to ensure an even distribution. Let the coffee steep for 3-5 minutes, depending on your preference, before enjoying.

And there you have it! Follow these steps and you'll have the best cup of coffee possible. Enjoy!

### Tips and Tricks

- Use filtered water for the best taste.
- Boil the water to 200 degrees Fahrenheit (93.3 degrees Celsius) for optimal extraction.
- Grind your coffee beans manually to control the consistency.
- Use a cool filter to prevent bitterness and enhance flavor.
- Pour the water into your cup by hand to evenly saturate the grounds.
